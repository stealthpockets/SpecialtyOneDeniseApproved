Use this tool to clear the cached market rates data and force a fresh fetch from the API.

Manual Instructions:

If the button doesn't work, you can manually clear the cache by:

  1. Open your browser's Developer Tools (F12)
  2. Go to the Console tab
  3. Paste this command: localStorage.removeItem('specialty_one_market_rates'); localStorage.removeItem('specialty_one_market_rates_v2');
  4. Press Enter
  5. Refresh your application page
